## 🌟 The 5 Golden Rules

Internalize these from Day 1. They'll save you a lot of stress.

| # | Rule | Why |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **1** | **Ask after 15 minutes of being stuck** | Wasting hours alone helps no one. Asking saves the team time too. |
| **2** | **Daily Slack report — NEVER skip** | Keeps your QA Lead in the loop without meetings. Even a quick "stuck on X" is fine. |
| **3** | **No question is stupid** | Every senior QA was confused at the start. Asking shows you care. |
| **4** | **Confusion in Week 1 is normal** | Logistics + delivery + ops + tech = a lot. It clicks around Day 7–10. |
| **5** | **Rest on weekends — burnout kills onboarding** | This is a marathon, not a sprint. Recharge so you can absorb. |
